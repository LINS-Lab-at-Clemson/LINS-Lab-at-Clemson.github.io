The Delaware River Basin—a critical water source for New York City—faces the challenge of balancing municipal supply, cold-water fisheries, and salinity intrusion. We developed a modular, LSTM-augmented [Pywr-DRB model](https://pywr-drb.github.io/Pywr-DRB/intro.html) to simulate water supply, stream temperature, and salt-front dynamics in one decision-support framework.

Using multi-objective policy search, we optimize reservoir release rules and examine proactive strategies for reducing thermal stress while sustaining the basin's other priorities.
