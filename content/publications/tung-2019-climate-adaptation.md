abstract = """This study develops a climate-adaptation algorithm that combines the six-step climate-change adaptation process with a structured climate-risk template. The framework links hazard, exposure, and vulnerability while guiding users through problem and objective definition, current and future risk analysis, and evaluation of adaptation options. An application shows how the approach can clarify governance concerns and stakeholder objectives, trace relationships among risk components, and identify strategies for reducing impacts. The resulting workflow is intended as a standardized decision-support procedure for climate-risk assessment."""
