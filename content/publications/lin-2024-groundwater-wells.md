abstract = """Groundwater wells are essential for monitoring and using groundwater, yet the United States has lacked a unified, standardized inventory of their characteristics and locations. The United States Groundwater Well Database addresses this gap by compiling more than 14.2 million state and federal well records spanning 1763–2023. Available as tables and vector points, the database includes attributes such as purpose, location, depth, and capacity and applies cross-verification procedures to improve reliability. It provides a national resource for studying how groundwater is accessed and managed across regions and sectors."""
