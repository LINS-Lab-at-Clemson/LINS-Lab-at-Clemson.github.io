abstract = """This study evaluates the spatial information most useful for physics-informed, machine-learning forecasts of typhoon inundation. Hydrodynamic simulations supplied training and testing data for neural-network, neuro-fuzzy, support-vector-machine, and hybrid support-vector-machine/multi-objective-genetic-algorithm models. Using rainfall gauges, radar rainfall, typhoon characteristics, and conventional areal rainfall estimates for Yilan County, Taiwan, the analysis identifies influential features and suitable time lags. The hybrid model produced the strongest forecasts when supplied with the most consequential inputs and their appropriate lag lengths."""
