Before joining Clemson University, Dr. Lin was a postdoctoral researcher at Virginia Tech and Cornell University. He holds a Ph.D. in Civil Engineering from Lehigh University and M.S. and B.S. degrees in Bioenvironmental Systems Engineering from National Taiwan University.

His research models human–water systems to understand interactions among the environment, infrastructure, technology, and society for smarter water planning and management. Outside of work, he enjoys badminton and hiking.
